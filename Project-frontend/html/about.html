Lee's Story

David Lee is a one of a kind reptile enthusiast. His journey started in West Delray, FL (named for being the Most Fun Small Town in the USA), where he grew up just a stone throw away from a canal. In this canal, lived the most amazing species David had ever seen, the crocodile. When David first laid eyes on the croc, he knew he wanted to be around this creature and anything that relates to it for the rest of his life. After school, David would stop by corner store to pick up a soda and a bag of potato chips and head over to the canal to watch the crocodiles from a distant. When David graduated from Atlantic Community High School, he started his career at the Palm Beach Zoo & Conservation Society. David took care of any reptilian he could get his hands on and soon became a local icon with his exuberating love of allthings scaly.

In 1987, David decided that loving reptiles alone was not cutting it for him. He moved his whole family (wife, 2 kids, and dog) to Tallahassee, where he started Lee’s Reptile Emporium. It started off as a small, word of mouth business run out of their 2 acre property, selling only frogs and snakes. People started to recognize David from his talks at the zoo and would go to his home to see the animals and, of course, end up taking one home. As David expanded his selection of reptiles he alsogot a commercial spot on the local radio station, then on TV, and in 2008 opened anonline store to bring his affection for these beautiful creatures nationwide.

On behalf of Lee’s Reptile Emporium, David invites you all onto his property to choose your next family member, browse our shop and start your own reptilian adventure.
